Fauna and flora

The fauna in Alaska is full of life. Alaska has many kinds of different animal species. The animals have a thick layer of fat or a fur which make Them survive the cold temperatures in Alaska. There is too Dusk and dawn is the best times to watch the wildlife.
So as the animals have to fit cold climate the flora has too. The flowers are build strong then they can handle the hefty winds. One of the flowers such as the pink-purple moss campion have adapted not to grow to tal because its more isolated for the hard winds when it's closer to the ground.
